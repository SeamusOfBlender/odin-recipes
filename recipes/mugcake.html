MugCake

This mugcake recipe is one that I made all the time back in Edinburgh, becuse you can make a ton of it at once, and it's delicious. You will need to take your time making it to ensure everything comes out just right.

You will need:

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Cocoa
  • Icing Sugar
  • Milk Chocolate
  • Milk
  • Sprinkles

How to make:

  1. Mix the flour, eggs, milk, and cocoa powder
  2. Put a chocolate inside the mixture to melt as it bakes
  3. Bake in the microwave for approx. 1-2 minutes
  4. Mix the icing sugar with water until the desired consistency
  5. Let the cake cool
  6. Top with icing sugar and sprinkles
