Chicken Pie

This chicken pie recipe is one that I made all the time back in edinburgh, becuse you can make a ton of it at once, and it's delicious You will need to take your time making it to ensure everything comes out just right.

You will need:

  • Puff pastry
  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • FLour
  • Stock
  • Herbs
  • Bacon

How to make:

  1. Cook the chicken, onion, and bacon with some herbs
  2. Boil the vegetables
  3. Make a roux with the butter, flour and milk
  4. Mix some stock into the roux
  5. Mix together the roux, veggies, and meat
  6. Roll out the pastry, line a dish with it, and fill the dish with the mixture
  7. Cook for 20 minutes or until the pastry is golden brown at 180C
  8. Eat it all at once like a fat pig
