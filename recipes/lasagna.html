lasagna

This lasagna recipe is one that I made all the time back in Edinburgh, becuse you can make a ton of it at once, and it's delicious. You will need to take your time making it to ensure everything comes out just right.

You will need:

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Mince meat
  • Vegetables
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Herbs
  • Stock
  • Tomato Puree
  • Tomata Passata
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese

How to make:

  1. Cook the meat and vegetables with some herbs
  2. Add some tomato puree and passata until a preferred consistency
  3. Make a roux with the butter, flour and milk
  4. Mix some stock into the roux
  5. In layers, add the meat, the ricotta, the pasta, and the roux. Repeat until dish is full or ingredients run empty
  6. Top with Cheddar Cheese
  7. Cook for approx. 20 minutes at 180C (fan) and rest for a few minutes before serving
